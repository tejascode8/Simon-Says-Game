Description

Simon Says is a fun and challenging memory game where your goal is to follow Simon's color sequence.

Test your memory and reflexes as you try to keep up with the increasing difficulty level.

Can you remember the order of colors and beat Simon at his own game?

How to Play

Start the game by pressing any key.

Simon will play a sequence of colors.

Remember the sequence.

Click or tap the colors in the same order as Simon played them.

If you make a mistake, the game will end, and your score will be displayed.

Press any key to restart the game.
